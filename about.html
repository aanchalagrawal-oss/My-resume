About Agrawal Dental Clinic

Your Trusted Dental Care Partner in Jalgaon

Our Journey

Founded over two decades ago, Agrawal Dental Clinic has been at the forefront of modern dentistry in Jalgaon. With state-of-the-art infrastructure and a patient-centric approach, our clinic ensures a stress-free and transparent dental experience.

Dr. Jitendra Agrawal, a renowned endodontist, and Dr. Neha Agrawal, a skilled cosmetic dentist, lead a team of dedicated professionals. We are committed to offering compassionate care and exceptional service to every individual.

Our philosophy revolves around continuous learning, use of cutting-edge technology, and tailoring treatments to suit each patient's unique needs. Whether it is preventive care or complex smile makeovers — your oral health is in safe hands.
